CHAPTER 00 /

Before We Knew

(spoiler: he knew first)

Every story has a part that happens before anyone is paying attention. Ours starts in a LINE group.

JULY 2023

Max leaves KOAN — almost

Max moved on to a new company. He left the office, the desk, the title — but somehow never left the KOAN PM LINE group. He even wondered, later, whether he should. He didn’t. Remember that.

JUNE 2024

Primrose arrives

A year later, Primrose joined KOAN — and with it, that same LINE group. Two names in one group chat. Neither of them thinking anything of it. Yet.

2 AUG 2024 · 4:26 PM

The screenshot

Then one Friday afternoon, Max messaged P’Amy about work. Mostly about work. One line slipped out — and history (well, Amy) kept the screenshot:

He noticed her before they had ever met. Where did he even see her? “กลุ่มไลน์ PM” — the PM LINE group, of course.

some stories start loudly. ours started with one message — and two years of patience.

CHAPTER 01 /

Fate Tries Twice

the universe kept booking tables for three

Before we ever planned anything, the universe planned it for us — twice.

18 DEC 2024 · 22:00

Sushiro, Emporium

Max made plans to catch up with P’Amy after far too long. Primrose had just finished the gym with Amy — and came along. He expected Amy. She expected Amy. Neither expected each other. Back then they were both still in relationships — ones neither of them was happy in. Three people around a sushi counter, and two of them had no idea this was the beginning of anything.

24 SEP 2025

Sukiya, Asoke

Nine months later, fate tried again — this time through P’Fluke. By now they were both newly single. A simple lunch opposite the KOAN office, and once again: neither of them knew the other would be there. Same surprise, different colleague. The universe was clearly trying.

CHAPTER 02 /

Becoming Friends

officially just friends. officially.

The third time, nothing was left to chance. Max made the plans himself.

8 MAR 2026

Pickleball at Asoke Sports Club

The first meeting that wasn’t an accident — because Max finally engineered one. Pickleball with Fern and friends as very useful chaperones.

11 APR 2026

Donki day

Max, Primrose, Fern — an afternoon at D-Sports Stadium, inside Donki Mall. A game day that mattered more than it looked.

12 APR 2026

First night out — Sonny, Ari

First nightclub together, with Fern, Karn and crew. The drive home turned eventful — a police checkpoint and all — but what made the night unforgettable happened quietly inside the car: Max reached over and gently stroked her hair, soft as anything. And Primrose began to wonder, more than ever before — friends… or something more?

right here is where ‘maybe’ started turning into ‘definitely’.

CHAPTER 03 /

The Turn

the three days that changed everything

Three days at the start of May. That’s all it took, in the end.

1 MAY 2026

The bolster-pillow night

Max invited her to swim at his condo. The first time they were ever truly alone. That night, he told her he liked her — in words so careful they slipped right past her. They fell asleep with a bolster pillow between them, because he refused to let her feel unsafe, not even for a second. But morning came, and the pillow lost the battle — he moved closer and wrapped his arms around her. Their first hug. Some walls come down slowly. This one came down at sunrise.

3 MAY 2026

Day One of Us

First workout together — Virgin Active, Siam Discovery. Then back to Sonny with Fern and P’Kong, where the night produced a plot twist: Max ran into Earth, a friend he hadn’t seen in five years. Fern was interested. Max played cupid. It worked a little too well — and suddenly the room was theirs alone. And in that room, Max told her he liked her — again. The same thing he’d tried to say two nights earlier, but this time in plain words, nothing that could slip past her. This time, she heard it. This time, she said yes to becoming more than friends. The night “us” began — the one we both call Day One of Us.
